About me

My name is Susanna, and I'm an aspiring front-end developer with a marketing background. In the spring of 2023, I made the decision to switch my career to front-end development. I am already familiar with technologies such as React, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, Bootstrap, APIs, multiple-page hosting, media queries, and Visual Studio Code. Through hands-on practice, I have integrated live APIs and created multiple-page websites.

The more I delved into it, the more my passion grew, and I developed a desire to learn more. I believe that my marketing background will help me in creating user-friendly and responsive websites.

Join me as I continue to push boundaries and make an impact in the world of front-end development.
